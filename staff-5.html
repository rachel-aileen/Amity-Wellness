Christina Kent

Christina is an exceptionally talented ultrasound technician driven by her deep-rooted passion for helping expectant mothers during pregnancy.

Considering the delicate nature of pregnancy, Christina understands that each ultrasound scan provides crucial insights into the health of both mother and baby.

She is known for her extraordinary attention to detail and empathetic bedside manner. Patients often feel at ease in her presence, as she takes the time to explain the procedure, answer questions, and alleviate any concerns. Christina's commitment to her craft extends beyond the clinic. She is an active member of several professional organizations, such as the American Institute of Ultrasound in Medicine (AIUM) and the Society of Diagnostic Medical Sonography (SDMS). In addition, she holds a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Science from the University of Maryland.
