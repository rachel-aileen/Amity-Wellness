Pap Smear

Average Fee: $300

A pap smear, also known as a pap test, is a crucial procedure to screen for cervical cancer. For women between the ages of 21-65, we recommend pap smears every three years. Regular pap smears are a vital part of preventive health care for women, and we recommend them as a part of your routine wellness plan.

Cost Includes:

  • Routine Pap Smear Screening
  • Adolescent Pap Smear Screening
  • HPV Co-Testing
  • Colposcopy
  • Cervical Biopsy
