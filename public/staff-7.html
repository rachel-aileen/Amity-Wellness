Elizabeth Scott

Elizabeth has over a decade of experience as a certified ultrasound technician specializing in women's health at our OBGYN clinic.

Building rapport with patients is an art, and Elizabeth strives at getting to know her patients on a personal level. Her warm demeanor helps ease any worries women often have during an ultrasound scan.

Elizabeth was raised by her grandmother, who battled a gynecological condition during the last years of her life. Her grandmother's relentness nature to continue living every day to its fullest inspired Elizabeth to pursue a career in gynecology. She is not just an expert in the technical aspects of her job but also a lifelong learner. In her spare time, Elizabeth volunteers at local women's health organizations and regularly participates in community outreach programs aimed at educating women about the importance of prenatal care and early detection of gynecological conditions.
